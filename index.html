WELCOME TO AUCTION SYSTEM

An online auction (or also electronic auction or e-auction ) is an auction that is held over the internet. Like auctions in general, online auctions come in a variety of types.

The scope and reach of these auctions have been propelled by the Internet to a level beyond what the initial purveyors had anticipated. This is mainly because online auctions break down and remove the physical limitations of traditional auctions such as geography, presence, time, space, and a small target audience. This influx in reachability has also made it easier to commit unlawful actions within an auction.
